Materials Sourcing

Wecyclers offers convenient household recycling service in densely populated low-income neighbourhoods using a fleet of low-cost cargo bikes and an SMS-based incentives platform.

Household Involvement

Households are encouraged to recycle plastic bottles and aluminium cans. For every kilogram of material that families recycle with us, they receive redeemable Wecyclers points over their mobile phones in return.

Wecyclers Incentives

For every kilogram of material that families recycle with us, they receive redeemable Wecyclers points over their mobile phones in return. Families can then redeem their points for good that they value, such as electronics and household goods.

Recycling Feedstock

After collection, Wecyclers aggregates material to sell to local recycling processors. Wecyclers alleviates the supply constraints faced by processors by providing a consistent supply of well-sorted, high quality recyclable material.

Our Mission

As a Wecycler, you are a part of a team on a mission to increase public health and show the world by doing good to the enviornment we all win!

Our Acomplishments

Since August 2012, Wecyclers has registered over 3,500 households for our collection service, built 15 collection cargo bikes, created over 20 jobs and collected and sold over 126 metric tons of recyclable materials.

Our Bright Future

We expect to grow our subscriber base to 1M over the next 5 years, collecting over 730,000 tons of waste.

Our Growth Strategy

We expect to achieve this ambitious growth strategy by franchising wecycles to micro entrepreneurs providing them with a dignified and safe source of livelihood.
