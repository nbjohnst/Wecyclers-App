Materials Sourcing

Wecyclers offers a household recycling service using a fleet of low-cost cargo bikes and an SMS-based incentives platform.

Household Involvement

For every kilogram of material recycled with us, families receive redeemable Wecyclers points over their mobile phones in return.

Wecyclers Incentives

Families can then redeem their points for goods such as electronics and household goods.

Recycling Feedstock

Wecyclers supplies well-sorted, high quality recyclable material to local recycling processors.

Our Mission

Our misison is to increase public health, create jobs and show the world a new way to Recycle!

Our Acomplishments

Wecyclers has registered over 3,500 house, built 15 collection cargo bikes, created over 20 jobs and collected over 126 metric tons of recyclables.

Our Bright Future

We expect to grow our subscriber base to 1M over the next 5 years, collecting over 730,000 tons of waste.
